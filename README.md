# Fantasy football holographic cards

### Controls

* Touch or drag across the surface of the cards to observe their holographic effects
* Press each card to cause them to additionally glow.
* Swipe or drag left and right to change cards within the deck.
* Download card images suitable for printing by using the download button in the top right
* Create your own deck of cards by clicking the edit button.
* Toggle between editing and viewing your deck using the buttons at the top left


### A collection of advanced CSS styles, applied with React.
Uses CSS Transforms, Gradients, Blend-modes and Filters to simulate the various Holofoil effects found
in pokemon cards but applied to new kinds of cards mediums.
